Ohio

Ohio City is a historic neighborhood located just west of downtown Cleveland, Ohio. Known for its vibrant community, the area features a mix of cultural heritage and modern amenities. Ohio City is home to the iconic West Side Market, a bustling public market with a wide array of fresh produce, artisanal goods, and local vendors. The neighborhood boasts a thriving food and craft beer scene, with numerous breweries, restaurants, and cafes. Its charming streets are lined with historic architecture, local shops, and green spaces, making it a popular destination for residents and visitors alike.
